How it works

TimeJump sniffs out a t (time) parameter in your URL. If it finds the parameter, TimeJump will attempt to fast-forward the podcast to the specified timeframe.

The following formats (in order of recommendation) are supported:

Media Fragments w/ hash:
http://mypodca.st/first-episode/#t=1:23:45
http://mypodca.st/first-episode/#t=23:45
http://mypodca.st/first-episode/#t=234
